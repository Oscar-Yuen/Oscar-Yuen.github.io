Story & Origin

The Pastel on Board version

The Pastel on Board Version is one of the five versions of The Scream and was created three years after the artisit's first arrival in Berlin.

Renowned for its exaggerated portrayal of a scream, The Scream stems from the artist's immense feeling of anxiety.

The Scream depicts a self-portrait of the artist: While Munch was walking with his two friends, the sun set. As the sky abruptly turned red, it aroused a lasting sadness in Munch. As he felt the blood red sky overwhelm the city while his friends went on, he was engulfed in anxiety and felt a fathomless scream penetrating.
